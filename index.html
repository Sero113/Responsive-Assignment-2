About Me

I am a sound engineer and producer from Toronto Canada, I create the perfect sound for any project.
With years of experience in the industry, I have developed a keen ear for detail and a deep understanding of the technical aspects of sound production. My expertise ranges between rock and hip-hop. I have worked with both established and up-and-coming artists, providing them with the tools and guidance they need to bring their vision to life. In addition to my technical skills, I pride myself on my ability to collaborate effectively with clients, ensuring that their creative vision is realized in every aspect of the production process.
